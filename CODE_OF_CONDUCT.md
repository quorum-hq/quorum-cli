# Code of Conduct

quorum-cli is open to contributors and users with a wide range of experience. The project began as a personal project; regardless of its size or history, we expect interactions here to meet the same standards of respect and professionalism as in any healthy open-source community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Focus disagreement on technical merit and evidence. Personal attacks are not acceptable.
- Welcome newcomers: ask and answer questions in good faith. There are no bad questions in this repository.
- Treat reports of real-world problems—including production issues—with seriousness and empathy.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, doxxing, deliberate trolling, threats of violence, sexualized content, or any conduct that would lead a reasonable person to feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Reports or concerns may be sent to [kachhwalvansh230@gmail.com](mailto:kachhwalvansh230@gmail.com). We aim to provide an initial response within **48 hours**.

## Formal policy

This document summarizes our expectations. The [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html), is adopted as the full Code of Conduct for this project. If anything here conflicts with the Covenant, the Covenant prevails.
